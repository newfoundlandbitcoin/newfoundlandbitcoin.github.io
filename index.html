WHAT IS BITCOIN?

Bitcoin is revolutionizing the way money is sent across the globe.

EASY PAYMENT SYSTEM

Bitcoin is a simple way to send and receive payments for both individuals and businesses alike. All that is required is an internet connection and a device running Bitcoin-compatible software.


FAST INTERNATIONAL PAYMENTS

Bitcoin transactions can be made across the planet in minutes. There is no bank to slow down the process, level outrageous fees, or freeze the transfer. You can receive money from a customer in your shop just as easily as you can take payment from a client in Europe.


WORKS ANYTIME, ANYWHERE

Unlike a traditional bank, the Bitcoin network is always open for business. The network operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year throughout the world so transactions can be processed at any hour without delay.


MOBILE PAYMENTS MADE EASY

Bitcoin allows your customers to pay with a simple two step scan-and-pay. There is no need for them to sign up, swipe a card, type a PIN, or sign anything. You simply display a QR code in your Bitcoin wallet application and let your customer scan it with their mobile device. Most mobile devices are supported including Android and Apple devices.


SECURITY AND CONTROL

Bitcoin transactions are secured by military grade cryptography. Nobody can charge you money or withdraw from your account without your consent. Bitcoin can give you control over your money and a strong level of protection against many types of fraud.


WHY ACCEPT BITCOIN?

Lower your expenses while making it easier for your customers to pay.

LOWEST FEES AVAILABLE

You can make and receive payments using the Bitcoin network with almost no fees. Why pay credit card companies 3% of your sales when you don't have to?


FRAUD PROTECTION

Any business that accepts credit cards or PayPal knows the problem of payments that are later reversed. Fraudulent charge-backs result in limited market reach and increased prices, which in turn penalizes customers. Bitcoin payments are irreversible and secure, meaning that the cost of fraud is no longer pushed onto your shoulders as a merchant.


SETTLEMENT OF FUNDS

A number of payment processors will allow you to accept Bitcoin from your customers while receiving settlement in Canadian Dollars, US Dollars, or another currency instead of receiving bitcoins. This allows you to add Bitcoin without disrupting your business.


NO PCI COMPLIANCE

Accepting credit cards online typically requires extensive security checks in order to comply with the PCI standard. Bitcoin still requires you to secure your wallet and your payment requests. However, you do not carry the costs and responsibilities that come with processing sensitive information from your customers such as credit card numbers.


INCREASED VISIBILITY

Bitcoin is an emerging market that is increasingly making headlines. Being among the growing number of businesses that accept Bitcoin is a great way to increase the visibility of your business and reach new customers.


IS IT RIGHT FOR MY BUSINESS?

Bitcoin has a solution for practically every type of business.

HOME-BASED BUSINESSES

Bitcoin is great for home-based businesses as it can work without requiring additional hardware or complicated wiring. If you have a laptop with an internet connection you can accept payments from clients right in your home.


BRICK AND MORTAR STORES

Accepting Bitcoin does not require any expensive changes or re-training of staff. It can work alongside your current point-of-sale software and customers can easily make payments from their mobile phones.


TRAVELLING SALES

If your place of work is mobile and you are making sales on the go then Bitcoin is for you. You can easily accept payments on your existing mobile device instead of worrying about carrying around a bulky card processor and finding somewhere to hook it up.


ONLINE BUSINESSES

Make it easy for your customers anywhere in the world to send you their money without having to jump over any hurdles. Whether your online business is a simple webpage, a popular piece of e-commerce shopping cart software or a custom-coded web application you can accept payments with Bitcoin.
